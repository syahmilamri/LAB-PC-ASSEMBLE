# LAB-PC-ASSEMBLE

Through learning PC assembly, I gained a better understanding of how each hardware component works together to form a complete computer system. I learned the importance of compatibility between parts such as the motherboard, CPU, and RAM. This experience also improved my confidence in handling computer hardware and showed me that assembling a PC requires patience, attention to detail, and proper safety practices. Overall, learning PC assembly helped me appreciate computer systems more and made me more confident in upgrading or troubleshooting a PC in the future.
